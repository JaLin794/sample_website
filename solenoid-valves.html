Stainless Steel Solenoid Valves

VIG analyzers use stainless steel solenoids and custom manifolds in sample-contact portions of the system.

Stainless steel construction is used wherever the sample comes into contact with the valve or manifold, helping support clean flow control and durable operation.

Service note: For valve replacement, provide the analyzer model and application so VIG can confirm the correct valve and manifold arrangement.
