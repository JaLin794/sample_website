THC Analyzer

Model 10 Hydrocarbon Analyzer

FID Single Channel, Spot Heated Total Hydrocarbon Analyzer

The Model 10 is a microprocessor-based, spot-heated total hydrocarbon gas analyzer designed for accurate, sensitive, and stable measurement.

It uses a flame ionization detector for continuous measurement of a dry sample stream at ambient temperature. The detector is spot heated to help prevent condensation and support repeatable performance across a broad range of hydrocarbon concentrations in gaseous mixtures or ambient air.

Best fit: Single-point total hydrocarbon measurement where the sample stream is dry and ambient-temperature operation is appropriate.

Standard Specifications
Measuring method
1x spot heated flame ionization detector (FID)
Measurement ranges (Four ranges per analyzer)

Standard ranges: 0-10, 0-100, 0-1000, and 0-10000 ppm with 0.01 ppm lower detection limit; or

0-1000, 0-10000, 0-100000, and 0-100000 ppm with 0.1 ppm lower detection limit. Other ranges are available upon request.

Zero and span noise
Less than 0.5% of full scale
Zero and span drift
+/- 1.5% full scale per 24 hours
Linearity
Within 1.5% of full scale through all ranges
Repeatability
Within 1.5% of full scale through all ranges
Stability
Within 1.5% of full scale through all ranges
Oxygen synergism
Within 1.5% of full scale within selected range
Response time
Within 5 seconds to 90% of final reading
Ambient temperature
50 degrees F to 120 degrees F
Flow rate
Standard: 4 L/min; or 10 L/min upon request
Physical dimensions
19 in. wide front panel; 16.75 in. wide chassis; 18 in. deep chassis; 21 in. deep with fittings and handles; 7 in. high
Weight
35 - 45 lbs, depending on options
Safety
Flame-out indicator lamp; flame-out alarm contact on back panel; fuel shut-off; calibration and zero solenoid shut-off; optional sample shut-off
Voltage output
Standard: 0-10 VDC; or 0-1 VDC or 0-5 VDC optional
Current output
4-20 mA, sourcing
Flame-out alarm
Normally open, low-current relay contact; closes on alarm; latching
Concentration 1 alarm
Normally open, low-current relay contact; closes on alarm; latching
Ignition
Automatic; can be set to manual by operator from front panel
Glow plugs
Main and spare glow plugs installed; selectable by switch on back panel
Warm-up time
Usable in approximately 45 minutes; stable in approximately 2 hours
Display
Graphic, backlit, 240W x 64H pixels, high contrast, wide viewing angle
Operational Requirements
Fuel
UHP hydrogen at 18 psi incoming pressure standard; UHP hydrogen/helium mixture at 18 psi incoming pressure optional
Combustion air
Oil/water/hydrocarbon-free instrument air at 18 psi incoming pressure
Zero calibration gas
UHP zero grade air or nitrogen at 9 psi incoming pressure
Span calibration gas
Known concentration of operator-selected hydrocarbons balanced in either air or nitrogen at 9 psi incoming pressure
Optional gases
UHP zero grade air or nitrogen for units with dilution option at 18 psi incoming pressure; oil/water/hydrocarbon-free instrument air for cleaning options at 50 psi incoming pressure
Power requirements
115VAC at 60 Hz at 720 W; optional 220 VAC at 50 Hz
