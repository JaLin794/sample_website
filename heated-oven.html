Component

Heated Oven

VIG heated analyzers include a temperature-controlled oven managed by the analyzer microprocessor.

Sample-contact components are kept inside the oven to help prevent moisture condensation and detector contamination. This controlled heated environment supports repeatable measurements in applications where sample temperature and condensation control matter.

Service note: Oven temperature configuration and serviced components vary by analyzer model.
