Custom Heated Sample Pumps

VIG offers cold-head and heated-head sample pumps for analyzer and sample system applications.

Available flow rates include 5.5 L/M and 14 L/M. Heated pump heads are manufactured by VIG and include interchangeable cartridge heaters for serviceable thermal control.

The pumps use PTFE diaphragms, PTFE flapper-type valves, and 316 stainless steel fittings to support clean sample handling and long-term reliability.

Service note: VIG can help select the correct pump configuration based on flow requirement, sample condition, and analyzer model.
