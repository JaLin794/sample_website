Component

PTFE Isolated Burner (FID)

VIG manufactures its own flame ionization detector burner assembly for analyzer applications where clean sample contact, stable ignition, and field serviceability are important.

Sample-exposed parts and related hardware are made from 316 stainless steel. The inner chamber is lined with PTFE to help reduce corrosion and contamination risk in the detector flow path.

Twist-type BNC electrical connections support secure contact, and the glow plug is positioned in the exhaust path to support reliable ignition. Capillaries are designed so customers can remove and replace them in the field.

Service note: Contact VIG to confirm burner compatibility with your analyzer model and sample conditions.
