About VIG Industries

VIG Industries manufactures, services, and rents environmental emissions monitoring equipment with a focus on FID-based hydrocarbon analyzer systems.

Our mission is to remain the customer's primary service provider before, during, and long after equipment delivery, supporting analyzer selection, startup, maintenance, repair, modifications, rentals, and ongoing reliability needs.

Since 1993, VIG Industries has supported customers with single and dual channel flame ionization detector (FID) based gas chromatography systems, volatile organic compound (VOC) monitors, total hydrocarbon (THC) analyzers, and related emissions monitoring equipment.

  • Manufactures and supports a complete product line of microprocessor-controlled hydrocarbon analyzers and combustion / zero air generators.
  • Provides scheduled maintenance, repair, troubleshooting, and long-term support for equipment already in the field.
  • Provides peripheral devices such as chart recorders / data loggers, programmable logic controllers (PLC), and precision components for VIG emission monitoring equipment and equipment from other manufacturers.
  • Helps customers evaluate monitoring needs, select analyzer configurations, and plan complete monitoring systems around site-specific requirements.
  • Supports Continuous Emissions Monitoring (CEM) system integration when a project calls for a broader monitoring solution.
  • Offers rental hydrocarbon analyzers for temporary needs and trade-in options toward new analyzer equipment.

VIG engineers are eager to assist with monitoring needs. Please contact us with questions, support requests, or equipment orders.
