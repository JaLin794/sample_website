FID Hydrocarbon Analyzers

Flame ionization detector analyzers for total hydrocarbon and volatile organic compound measurement applications.

Total Hydrocarbon Analyzers (THC)

THC Analyzer

Model 10

Single channel | Spot heated | THC

Microprocessor-based FID analyzer for continuous dry sample measurement at ambient temperature. The detector is spot heated to help prevent condensation and support repeatable performance.

THC Analyzer

Model 10/2

Dual channel | Spot heated | THC

Dual-channel version of the spot-heated FID analyzer, using two detectors for continuous measurement of two independent dry sample streams.

THC Analyzer

Model 20

Single channel | Oven heated | THC

Oven-heated FID analyzer designed to continuously measure one point. Sample-contact components are kept in a temperature-controlled oven to help prevent condensation.

THC Analyzer

Model 20/2

Dual channel | Oven heated | THC

Dual-point oven-heated FID analyzer with two detectors, sample filters, sample lines, valves, pump heads, and capillaries maintained in a heated oven.

Volatile Organic Compound (VOC) Analyzers

VOC Analyzer

Model 200

Dual channel | Oven heated | Methane / Non-methane / Total

FID NMHC analyzer using one detector for real-time total hydrocarbons and a second detector with a GC column to separate methane and non-methane components.

VOC Analyzer

Model 200/1

Single FID | Oven heated | Methane / Non-methane

Methane/non-methane hydrocarbon analyzer that measures at preset intervals, using a GC column and one FID to separate and read methane and non-methane components.

VOC Analyzer

Model 200T

Oven heated | Methane / Non-methane / Total

FID NMHC analyzer configured for methane, non-methane, and total hydrocarbon measurement in oven-heated applications.

VOC Analyzer

Model 210

Dual FID | Oven heated | Methane / Ethane / NMEHC / Total

Oven-heated analyzer for methane, ethane, non-methane-ethane hydrocarbons, and total hydrocarbons. One FID supports speciated readings while the second continuously measures total hydrocarbons.

Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems (CEMS)

Integrated emissions monitoring systems built around analyzer, control, data, and support equipment for continuous measurement applications.

Custom Integrated Systems

VIG provides CEMS integration services for applications that require analyzers, control panels, chart recorders, data acquisition, sequencing, wiring, and support equipment to operate as a complete monitoring package.

Analyzer-Based CEMS

Systems can incorporate VIG hydrocarbon analyzers such as Model 20, Model 200/1, dual-channel analyzers, multipoint sequencers, flow control panels, data logging equipment, and combustion / zero air generators.

Application-Specific Builds

CEMS packages may be designed for abatement efficiency, VOC destruction control, carbon adsorption control, process monitoring, stack monitoring, or replacement of outdated monitoring systems.
