Continuous Emission Monitoring Systems (CEMS)

Integrated emissions monitoring systems built around analyzer, control, data, and support equipment for continuous measurement applications.

Custom Solutions

Custom Integrated Systems

VIG provides CEMS integration services for applications that require analyzers, control panels, chart recorders, data acquisition, sequencing, wiring, and support equipment to operate as a complete monitoring package.

Analyzer-Based CEMS

Systems can incorporate VIG hydrocarbon analyzers such as Model 20, Model 200/1, dual-channel analyzers, multipoint sequencers, flow control panels, data logging equipment, and combustion / zero air generators.

Application-Specific Builds

CEMS packages may be designed for abatement efficiency, VOC destruction control, carbon adsorption control, process monitoring, stack monitoring, or replacement of outdated monitoring systems.
